About

#Research Interests

My research focuses on transportation electrification policy and planning, sustainable transportation systems, and the interdependence of transportation and energy sectors. With the application of AI methods in transportation domain, she aims to explore the nexus between building energy systems and electrified transportation, with the goal of enhancing community resilience and mitigating health impacts during climate change events and power outages.
